  - question: |
      I love the piece!  Can you tell us about your family's Jewish story?
    answer: |
      Thank you!  My mother's grandparents came to New York from Austria-Hungary in the early 1900's, and most of my family was born and raised in NY with strong ties to the Jewish community.  One story: my grandfather Lee, and his father, Max were each individually collecting illegal weapons in 1947-48 to smuggle to Israel, where Max's brother was a soldier, but neither one told the other what he was doing.  Then, one day, Max picked his father's satchel and asked why it was so heavy.  My grandpa tried to play it off as a bunch of books, but the bag was far too heavy to be just books.  That's how they learned that they were both collecting weapons in secret.
  - question: |

      My mother ensured my and I grew up in a Jewish home, celebrating all the holidays and attending synagogue each week for most of my childhood.  I also went to Jewish day school from kindergarten through 8th grade, as well as attending Sunday school.
