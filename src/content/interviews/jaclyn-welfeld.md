  - question: |
      Tell us about your experience with TPF.
    answer: |
      I've had two different mentors, as I originally started while in college with Mrs. Mariel Goldstein (of Meor Maryland), then switched to a mentor from my area (Mrs. Adinah Swiatycki of Etz Chaim Baltimore) when I got home.

      I really like the opportunity TPF gives to get close to a mentor with whom to converse and interact in a truly deep and meaningful way.  At this point, I am lucky enough to consider myself my mentor's friend.

      I've referred others to TPF.  I no longer meet one one-on-one with the mentor; rather, we are a group of three, myself, a friend and Mrs Swiatycki.  We all get together to discuss the podcast and our takeaways.  I sometime take notes on the podcast content itself, but usually I prepare by writing down my specific takeaways.  My friend does an amazing job with summarizing the podcast so I usually let her begin and then I get to fill in the blanks and add my own commentary.
