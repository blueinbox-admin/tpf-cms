  - question: |

      I really enjoyed Habit of Happiness.

      (Note from Mrs. Swiatycki: Ilan generally selects short, to-the-point podcasts and finds direct ways to apply them to his life.  He regularly takes a message of strength and emotional/spiritual resilience from the podcast discussions; not to let circumstances you can't control throw you off-balance.)
  - question: |

  - question: |

      It's a way to connect to G-d and my roots.  I'm going through some hard times right now and thought maybe it would give me some light.
  - question: |
      May the tefillin indeed bring you light.  Have you witnessed antisemitism since October 7th, 2023?

      It’s definitely been very hard seeing how non-Jewish people turn on us.  It's especially hard to see how they believe everything people say on social media.  I’m lucky that I work with a lot of Jewish people, so it hasn’t really affected me, other than losing a couple “friends.”
